Meta Ads Interview Questions

Basic

Q: What is CTR?
A: Percentage of users who clicked after seeing the ad.

Q: What is CPM?
A: Cost per 1000 impressions.

Intermediate

Q: What is Lookalike Audience?
A: Audience created using existing customer data to find similar users.

Q: What is Learning Phase?
A: Period where Meta algorithm learns to optimize delivery.

Advanced

Q: Why high CPM happens?
A: Poor creatives, high competition or narrow audience.

Q: How to scale campaign?
A: Increase budget gradually or duplicate winning ad sets.
