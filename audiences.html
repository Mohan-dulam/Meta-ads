Meta Ads Audiences

1. Core Audience

Interest based targeting using demographics, interests and behaviors.

Example:

Women 23-40 in Hyderabad interested in skincare, beauty salons and cosmetics.

  • Location targeting
  • Age & gender
  • Interests
  • Behaviors (online shoppers)

2. Custom Audience (Retargeting)

  • Website visitors
  • Instagram engagers
  • Video viewers 50%+
  • Lead form openers
  • Customer database upload

Most converting audience in Meta advertising.

3. Lookalike Audience

Meta finds people similar to converters.

  • 1% Lookalike → High quality
  • 3% Lookalike → Balanced
  • 5% Lookalike → Scale
