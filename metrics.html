Meta Ads Metrics Explained

  • Reach: Unique users
  • Impressions: Total views
  • CTR: Click-through rate
  • CPC: Cost per click
  • CPM: Cost per 1000 impressions
  • CPA: Cost per acquisition
  • ROAS: Revenue / Ad spend
  • Frequency: Average ad exposure per user
