Campaign Optimization Framework

When to Optimize

  • After 72 hours
  • After minimum impressions (1500+)

Diagnosis Guide

High CPM:
  • Narrow audience
  • Poor relevance score
Low CTR:
  • Weak creative hook
  • Irrelevant messaging
High CPA:
  • Wrong audience
  • Poor landing page
  • Incorrect objective

Creative Testing

  • Test 3 creatives
  • Test 2 audiences
  • Test 2 copies

Scaling

  • Increase budget 20% every 48 hours
  • Duplicate winners
  • Expand lookalikes
