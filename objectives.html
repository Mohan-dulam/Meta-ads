Campaign Objectives & KPI Mapping

The campaign objective instructs Meta algorithm what outcome to optimize for. Meta does not show ads to everyone â€” it predicts which users are most likely to complete the chosen action.

Awareness Objective

Meta shows ads to users likely to remember the ad.

Use When:
  • New brand
  • Store opening
  • Brand recognition campaigns
KPI: Reach, CPM, Ad recall lift

Traffic Objective

Optimizes for link clicks or landing page views.

KPI: CPC, CTR, Landing page views

Engagement Objective

Optimizes for likes, comments, shares and post interactions.

KPI: Engagement rate, Cost per engagement

Lead Generation Objective

Optimizes for form submissions.

KPI: CPL (Cost per lead), Lead quality

Sales Objective

Optimizes for purchase event using pixel.

KPI: CPA, ROAS, Conversion rate

Important Concept

Meta learns from behavior patterns. If you select Traffic but expect leads, the algorithm will find clickers, not buyers.
