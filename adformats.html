Meta Ad Formats & Ratios

  • Image Ad – 1:1 (1080x1080)
  • Feed Video – 4:5 (1080x1350)
  • Stories/Reels – 9:16 (1080x1920)
  • Carousel – Multiple cards
  • Collection – Ecommerce catalog

How Meta Chooses Placement

flowchart LR
AdCreative --> PlacementPrediction
PlacementPrediction --> BestPlacement
BestPlacement --> UserFeed

Example

If user watches reels frequently → Meta shows your ad in reels instead of feed.
