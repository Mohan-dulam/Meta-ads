Campaign Optimization Guide

Learning Phase

Meta needs ~50 conversions per week to exit learning.

When to Optimize

  • After 3–4 days
  • After enough impressions

Common Fixes

  • High CPC → Improve creative
  • Low CTR → Change hook
  • High CPA → Change audience
  • High Frequency → Creative fatigue

Scaling Strategy

  • Increase budget 20% every 2–3 days
  • Duplicate winning ad sets
  • Expand lookalikes
