Real Optimization & Scaling Strategy

When NOT to Optimize

  • Within first 48 hours
  • During learning phase

Diagnosis Method (Professional Approach)

Step 1: Check CPM → Audience Issue

Step 2: Check CTR → Creative Issue

Step 3: Check Conversion Rate → Landing Page Issue

Correct Scaling Methods

Vertical Scaling

Increase budget 10–20% every 48 hours.

Horizontal Scaling

Duplicate winning ad set into:
  • New lookalike audiences
  • Broad audience
  • Different creatives

Creative Scaling

Launch new creatives every 10–14 days to avoid fatigue.

Why Scaling Fails

  • Increasing budget too fast resets learning
  • Audience too small
  • Only one creative running
